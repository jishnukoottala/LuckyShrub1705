About Lucky Shrub

Where Passion Meets Creativity

Lucky Shrub was founded by Jason and Maria, a husband and wife team who have shared a deep love for plants for many years. Their combined passion for gardening and landscaping has blossomed into a thriving business dedicated to transforming spaces into beautiful, green oases.

With an eye for detail and a creative spirit, Jason leads the design and landscaping projects at Lucky Shrub. As the garden architect, he creates stunning, personalized garden designs that reflect each client's unique vision. Jason oversees every aspect of the design process and manages his skilled team of landscapers to ensure each project is executed to perfection.
